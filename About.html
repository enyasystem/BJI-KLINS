About Us

BJI KLINS Cleaning Service
Executive Summary
BJI KLINS, a professional cleaning service company, is dedicated to providing exceptional cleaning experiences for residential and commercial clients in Warri City and anywhere within Nigeria. We offer a variety of customizable cleaning services, including fumigation, to meet the specific needs of our clients, ensuring a clean, healthy, and organized environment. Our focus on reliability, eco-friendly products, and exceptional customer service positions BJI KLINS as a premier cleaning service.

Company Overview
BJI KLINS is a local cleaning service company, established since 2019. We are committed to providing high-quality cleaning solutions with a personalized touch. Our team is comprised of experienced, reliable, and insured cleaners, along with licensed and certified fumigation technicians.
